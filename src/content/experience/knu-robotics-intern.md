- Worked under **Dr. Yuvaraj** on autonomous-robotics research (KNU PIAI Lab × VIT Vellore collaboration).
- Built **HazPatrol** — autonomous coverage of unknown areas using a modified **C\*** algorithm that incrementally builds a Reachability Connectivity Graph (RCG) and drives boustrophedon laps until the full navigable area is covered.
- Implemented a **ROS 2 gas-source localization** stack (tested in GADEN) with CUSUM-based hotspot detection, real-time concentration mapping, and Bayesian source estimation — used to produce the results for the paper.
